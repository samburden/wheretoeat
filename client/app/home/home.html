Where To Eat?

The Where To Eat? application is a tool to help you determine where to eat.

Yelp Integration

The application provides integration with Yelp so you can search for restaurants in your area. You can also save your favorite restaurants so you have quick access to them later.

Restaurant Picker

Allow the application to select a restaurant for you to eat at. The application can pick a restaurant from your personal list or let it search for restaurants in your area using Yelp.

Reports

See where you have eaten lately and what types of restaurants you prefer. You can even see which days of the week you eat out most often.
